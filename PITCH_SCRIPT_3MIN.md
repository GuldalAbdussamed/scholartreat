# ScholarTreat — 3-Minute Pitch Script (Simple English)

> Speak slowly and clearly. Each slide ≈ 30–40 seconds. Total ≈ 3 minutes.
> Words in **bold** are the key points — say them with energy.

---

## Slide 1 — Cover (≈ 20 sec)

"Hello everyone. We are Team 7.
Our project is **ScholarTreat** — AI-powered micro-scholarships on Stellar.
In short: a sponsor types one sentence, and a student gets real money in seconds."

---

## Slide 2 — The Solution (≈ 40 sec)

"Here is how it works.
A **sponsor** posts a small reward — a coffee, a textbook, or a mini-scholarship.
A **student** with a .edu email applies.
Then our **Gemini AI agent** reads what the sponsor wants in plain words, and ranks all the students.
The best match gets paid **instantly in USDC on Stellar** — no middlemen, no paperwork.
One command. Real on-chain money. Verified students. AI picks the winner."

---

## Slide 3 — The Problem (≈ 35 sec)

"Why does this matter?
**More than 200 million students** around the world need small amounts of money — ten to two hundred dollars — for tools, courses, or just to keep going.
Normal scholarships take months of paperwork and only pay out thousands.
And companies *want* to help, but they have no simple, trusted way to do it.
For example: a student in Istanbul needs just **15 dollars** for an online course. No card, no bank — only a .edu email. ScholarTreat solves this in **3 minutes, on-chain.**"

---

## Slide 4 — Technical Workflow (≈ 45 sec)

"Now the technical part — and everything here is **autonomous**.
One: the sponsor signs up. We auto-create a Stellar wallet and swap XLM to USDC on the Stellar DEX.
Two: the student verifies a .edu email, and gets a wallet too.
Three: the sponsor types a natural command, and **Gemini ranks every applicant**.
Four: our server sets the trustlines and sends **real USDC on Stellar** — and returns a transaction hash.
**Why Stellar?** Fast finality in five seconds, fees under one tenth of a cent, native USDC, and a built-in exchange. Agent payments only work because Stellar settles at machine speed."

---

## Slide 5 — The Team (≈ 25 sec)

"Finally, who we are.
We are builders who believe **money should never block education.**
We shipped ScholarTreat end-to-end in **36 hours** here at Build on Stellar, Istanbul.
We know the tech — full-stack, AI, and blockchain — and we know the problem, because we have been students who needed that small help.
Thank you. We are happy to show you a **live demo.**"

---

## Quick Q&A backup answers

- **Is it safe?** Students are .edu-verified; every payment is a real on-chain transaction you can check on Stellar.Expert.
- **What is the AI doing?** Gemini 2.5 Flash reads the sponsor's plain-language rule and scores each applicant, with an explanation.
- **What is x402?** Our agentic side-track: the AI scoring runs through a pay-per-call endpoint, showing a self-paying autonomous agent.
- **Why not a bank?** Banks are slow and expensive for tiny cross-border amounts. Stellar settles in seconds for almost zero fees.
